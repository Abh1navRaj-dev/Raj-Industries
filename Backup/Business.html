Our Business Portfolio

Discover the sectors and ventures driving Raj Industries' growth and innovation.

Raj Industries is developing a diversified portfolio of operating companies across key sectors. As a visionary startup venture, we are committed to building specialized brands that excel in innovation, quality, and market leadership. Our current focus areas include digital media, automotive, technology, and emerging innovation sectors.

Digital Media

In the digital media space, we aim to create engaging platforms and content that connect audiences worldwide. Our planned ventures will leverage cutting-edge technology to deliver immersive experiences, from streaming services to interactive media solutions.

Automotive

Our automotive initiatives focus on sustainable and innovative mobility solutions. We are exploring electric vehicles, autonomous driving technologies, and smart transportation systems to address the evolving needs of modern transportation.

Technology

Technology is at the core of our operations. We are developing solutions in AI, software development, cybersecurity, and emerging tech to drive digital transformation across industries and enhance everyday life.

Emerging Innovation Sectors

Beyond traditional sectors, we are incubating ideas in renewable energy, biotechnology, and other forward-thinking areas. Our goal is to foster disruptive innovations that create long-term value and positive impact.
